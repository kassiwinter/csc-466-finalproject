                                                  George Soros in May                  Photo:            Francois Mori/Associated Press                                                                                                                                  Updated Oct. 22, 2018 11:23 pm ET                      An explosive device was found Monday afternoon at the home of billionaire and Democratic donor         George Soros    in New York’s Westchester County, according to law-enforcement officials.     The Bedford Police Department said it received a call from the residence regarding a suspicious package.      An employee at Mr. Soros’s residence found the package, revealing what appeared to be an explosive device, police said. The employee put the package in a wooded area until police arrived.     A police bomb squad deactivated the explosive device, authorities said.               Police were at the scene investigating Monday evening. They have deemed the area safe, a spokesman said.
